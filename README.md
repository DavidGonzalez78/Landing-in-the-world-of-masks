
## ATERRIZAJE EN EL MUNDO DE LAS MÁSCARAS (LANDING IN THE WORLD OF MASKS)

"Aterrizaje en el mundo de las máscaras" is a videogame developed for Global Game Jam Barcelona 2026. The whole project was developed in under 48 hours, following the jam's theme: "MASK".

An extraterrestrial being has an accident and crashes its spaceship on an unknown planet. To find help and repair its vehicle, it must explore this mysterious world and use the power of different masks to return to space.

Made with: Unity, C#, Aseprite, Concepts.

The whole game can also be played online at [itch.io](https://daviddev78.itch.io/aterrizaje-forzoso).
